About Namaskar Telecom

A family-run mobile store, serving our neighbourhood since 2012 — built on trust, honest pricing, and friendly service.

Our story

A neighbourhood store, built on relationships

Founded by Chetan Satra, Namaskar Telecom opened its doors in 2012 as a small shop in the heart of the local market. What started with a handful of feature phones has grown into a fully-stocked store with every leading smartphone brand, smartwatches, true-wireless audio, and a service desk that handles everything from screen guards to data transfer.

But what hasn't changed is how we treat our customers. Every person who walks in is greeted by name when we know them, given honest advice — even when it means recommending a cheaper phone — and helped long after the purchase is done.

What we stand for

Three promises we keep, every single day

Honest pricing

Sticker price = final price. No hidden charges, no last-minute "package" deals. What you see is what you pay.

100% genuine stock

Every phone is sealed-box with India warranty, billed under GST. We never deal in grey market or refurbished as new.

We're here after the sale

Settings, transfers, screen issues, service co-ordination — you can walk in any day and we'll sort it out.

Milestones

A small journey, twelve years long

  • 2012 — The beginningChetan Satra opened the store with a single counter and 30 feature-phone models. First customer was the neighbour two shops down.
  • 2015 — Smartphones take overBecame authorised dealers for Samsung & Xiaomi. Set up our first in-store demo unit.
  • 2018 — Service desk launchedStarted in-house screen-guard application, data transfer, and warranty co-ordination services.
  • 2021 — EMI & exchangeTied up with Bajaj Finserv, HDFC and ICICI to offer no-cost EMI. Launched our exchange programme.
  • 2024 — Going onlineCrossed 1,000+ WhatsApp orders a year. Customers across nearby colonies now order directly through us.
  • 2026 — Hello, neighbourLaunched this website. The next chapter is online — but the store stays exactly where you know it.
