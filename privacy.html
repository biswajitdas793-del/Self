Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026. Plain-English summary of what we collect and why.

What we collect

When you fill in the enquiry form on the Contact page, we collect:

  • Name and phone number — so we can reply to you.
  • Optional message — the model or accessory you're interested in.

That's it. We don't ask for emails, addresses or payment details on this website. We don't use analytics or advertising cookies. The site is read-only browsing for visitors; sales and EMI happen in-store or over WhatsApp.

Why we collect it

Only to respond to your enquiry — usually a WhatsApp reply with availability and pricing. We don't share, sell or use your details for marketing campaigns.

Where it's stored

Enquiries are stored securely in our Supabase database, hosted in the Mumbai (ap-south-1) region. Only the shop owner can read them — they are not visible to anyone else, including site visitors.

Cookies

The public website doesn't set tracking cookies. The owner portal at /admin.html uses a Supabase session cookie strictly for keeping the owner signed in — this never applies to visitors.

WhatsApp links

Tapping any WhatsApp button on the site opens a chat with us on WhatsApp. WhatsApp's own privacy terms apply once you start the chat — we receive your phone number and the message text just as you'd expect from any WhatsApp conversation.

External links

Some product cards include "Compare on Flipkart" / "Compare on Amazon" links. Clicking them takes you to those external sites — we don't share anything with them; you simply land on their search page.

Your control

You can ask us to delete your enquiry record at any time — message us on WhatsApp at +91 80822 20143 with the phone number you used, and we'll remove it.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle data, we'll update this page and bump the "Last updated" date above.
